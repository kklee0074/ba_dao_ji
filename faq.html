Frequently Asked Questions

What is the NAS Tip Bot?

NAS Tip Bot is a Nebulas powered smart contract and service that allows users to easily send NAS micropayments over social media.

To be able to send tips on social media you will need to first register for a NAS Tip Bot account. You will then be able to invoke the bot by issuing the appropriate command in a post or comment on one of our integrated social networks.

Log In With Reddit

I've received a tip. How do I access it?

In order to withdrawal tips you have received you must first register for a NAS Tip Bot account. This is accomplished by logging into the Nas Tip Bot website using your Reddit account and then completing the registration process by calling our Smart Contract. This process will link your Reddit account with your NAS Wallet Address. After this registration has been completed you can withdrawal your funds anytime.

How do I send a tip on Reddit?

To be able to send tips on Reddit you will need to first register for a NAS Tip Bot account and then authorize our bot to be able to send tips on your behalf. Once these steps have been completed you can invoke the bot on Reddit using the command !tipnas <amount> anywhere in a comment on the /r/nebulas or /r/nasdev subreddits.

All tips are irreversible and do not need to be claimed by the recipient. Requests to reverse transactions will be denied since once a transaction is sent through the blockchain there is no way to reverse it.

Why do I have to keep re-authorizing the bot when I want to send tips?

This is an added security feature to ensure that you will always remain in full control of your deposited funds.

When will support for other social networks be added?

Support is currently planned for Slack, Telegram and Twitter. More information will be released shortly on our progress. If you wish to suggest an alternate social network please contract us.

How long does it take for tips to be delivered?

It shouldn't take the NAS Tip Bot more then a few minutes to confirm your tip and notify the recipient. If over 30 minutes has passed and you have not been messaged by the bot, please contact us and we will look into it.

What system limits are currently in place with the NAS Tip Bot?

A number of limitations are imposed on the system in order to increase security and to stop the chances of abuse.

Minimum tip: 0.001 NAS

Maximum tip: 5 NAS

Max number of Tips: 10 per day

Deposit limit: Max 5 NAS per day

Withdrawal limit: Max 5 NAS per day

To prevent loss of funds in case of a security breach, all funds are always stored safely on the blockchain in a smart contract and are never held in any wallets owned by NASTipBot.com.

This website is not connected and is independant from the part of the application that interacts with the Nebulas ledger.

Is there a fee for using the NAS Tip Bot?

A 1% (one percent) fee is charged on withdrawals to help cover the running costs of the bot which includes server costs and the transaction fees of moving funds around in the smart contract when tips are performed. These fees will also help to fund future expansion of the NAS Tip Bot platform and will allow integration into other social networks at a faster pace.
